Our Business Models

Rooftop Projects

CAPEX
Capital Expenditure

CAPEX is a common business model for solar deployment in India where the consumer purchases the solar PV system, by making 100% of the payment upfront or financing the system, often through a bank.

OPEX
Operational Expenditure

OPEX model is where the RESCO developer invests in solar rooftop asset and sells the generated power to the building owner in favour of a lower solar power tariff. The excess power may be sold by the building owner to the utility, according to the power purchase agreement through net metering system.
